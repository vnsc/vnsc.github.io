Newsletter

The all colour newsletter arrives six times a year and features the new web based landscape format allowing larger pictures of detailed items.  It usually varies in size from 12 to 20 pages and is downloaded via the members only Yahoo site. 

Each issue covers a variety of information, tips and ideas pertaining to N scale Victorian and Australian outline.  The clubs monthly meetings are also reported. How to articles on super detailing of locos, building of structures, maintenance tips, steam loco construction have appeared in recent issues.

Reviews on new products and manufacturers are also included. Most of the articles are contributed by members who are encouraged to write-up any aspect of their modelling activities for the interest and enjoyment of fellow members.

Six Issues per year only costs AUD$25.00  and includes membership to the Victorian N Scale Collective plus all the benefits of a special Yahoo page.
