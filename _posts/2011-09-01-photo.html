It’s past the morning peak and Wallan’s yard is a hive of activity with an early model T class making up the rake of wagons destined for the Heathcote line and Melbourne yards.

The multi-unit F and W class are awaiting their next call of duty as an Up Derm with ‘C’ van pulls into the platform for the waiting passengers.
