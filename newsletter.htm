The all colour newsletter arrives six times a year and features a web based landscape format allowing larger pictures of detailed items. It usually consists of 20 pages and is downloaded via the members only Groups.io website.

Each issue covers a variety of information, tips and ideas pertaining to N scale Victorian and Australian outline. The Club's monthly meetings are also reported. How to articles on super detailing of locomotives, building of structures, maintenance tips, locomotive and rolling stock construction are just some of the topics covered in recent issues.

Reviews on new products and manufacturers are also included. Most of the articles are contributed by members who are encouraged to write-up any aspect of their modelling activities for the interest and enjoyment of fellow members.

The membership fee of $25 includes the Newsletter and all the benefits of the Groups.io website.
